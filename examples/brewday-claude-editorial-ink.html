Brewday is a small-batch coffee subscription built for developers: single-origin beans roasted the same week you deploy, ground for your brewer, and delivered on a cadence you set — weekly, biweekly, or every sprint. Think of it as a CI pipeline for caffeine: it runs on schedule, it never breaks the build, and the output is always warm.
